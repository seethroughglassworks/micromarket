How It Works

In four easy steps you can have your product listed for buyers across the US. MicroMarket charges a monthly subsciption and a small listing fee once your product sells.

Commitment to Quality

Every listing includes a spec sheet, SDS, and certificate of analysis so buyers know exactly what they're getting. Quantities, packaging type, and pickup or freight details are all handled directly between buyer and seller.

Selling Requirements

To buy or sell on Micro Market, your operation must hold a valid FDA registration, an active state feed license, and a physical facility - no brokers or traders allowed. Every account and listing is manually reviewed before going live.
